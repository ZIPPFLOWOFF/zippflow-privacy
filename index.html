ZIPPFLOW Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect your name (entered during onboarding) stored only on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Files You Upload

When you use conversion, compression, or watermark features, your media files are temporarily uploaded to our processing server. Files are permanently deleted from our servers within 1 hour. We do not store, analyze, or share your files.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements. Google may collect device identifiers to serve relevant ads. See policies.google.com for details.

4. In-App Purchases

We use Google Play billing for Premium subscriptions. We do not store payment information. All payments are handled by Google.

5. Permissions

Storage/Photos: Required to select and save media files. Internet: Required to upload files and display ads.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.

7. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13.
